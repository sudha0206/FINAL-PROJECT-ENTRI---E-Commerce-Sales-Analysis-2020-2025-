
E-Commerce Sales Analysis (2020–2025)

This project presents an end-to-end **E-Commerce Sales Analysis** using Python, covering the years **2020 to 2025**.
It includes data cleaning, preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and insights generation to understand sales patterns, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency.

---

**Project Domain**

**Retail & E-Commerce Analytics**

This dataset captures online and in-store retail transactions, including orders, products, customers, payments, discounts, shipping, and returns.

---

**Project Objectives**

* Identify **sales trends** (yearly, monthly, seasonal) from 2020–2025
* Understand **customer purchase behavior** across countries & channels
* Analyze **product performance**, demand, profitability
* Evaluate **discount impact**, **shipping providers**, and **returns**
* Create meaningful **visualizations** and generate **actionable insights**
* Provide **recommendations** for improving sales & operational efficiency

---

**Dataset Details**

* **Rows:** 49,782
* **Columns:** 17
* **Source:** Kaggle
* **Timeline:** 2020–2025
* **Dataset Types:** Product info, customer info, payment, pricing, discount, shipping, returns
  

**Technologies Used**

* **Python**
* **Pandas, NumPy**
* **Matplotlib, Seaborn**


---

 **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing**

Key steps:

1. Handling missing values

2. Removing negative quantities & prices

3. Fixing duplicate entries

4. Standardizing text values

5. Treating outliers using **percentile capping**

6. Creating new features:

* `TotalSales`
* `TotalAmount`
* `SalesPerOrder`
* Date columns (Year, Month, Day, Weekday)

---

**Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

The analysis includes:

* Year-wise & Month-wise Sales Trends
* Country-wise Revenue
* Top Performing Products & Categories
* Payment Method Preferences
* Shipping Provider Performance
* Return Rate Analysis
* Customer Segmentation Insights

---

**Key Insights**

* Apparel & Electronics are the **top-selling** categories
* Most orders come from **Online** channel
* UPS & Royal Mail handle major shipments
* Discounts strongly affect quantity purchased
* Return rates vary by product and shipping provider
* Sales show strong **monthly & seasonal** trends

 **Outcome**

This project provides a complete understanding of e-commerce sales operations:

* Identified profitable categories & countries
* Evaluated impact of discount & shipping
* Improved customer behavior understanding
* Prepared dataset for dashboarding & machine learning
